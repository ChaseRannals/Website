The types of cars that I like.

In this website I will talk alittle about what kind of cars I like and why.

First up is the 1970 Camero SS.
  • I love the body lines in the car as well as how wide the car looks.
  • I like that most of these cars are still original with the exception of a few safety upgrades.
  • I love the stance of the car. A little higher in the back seems to make it look alittle more aggressive.
Second up is the 1967 Chevy C10 Pickup.
  • My first vehicle I ever bought.
  • Loved the way it drove and rode. Seemed like I was floating on air with the independent suspension.
  • Overall loved the look of the stock height and rally rims with beauty rings.
